# design-author rubric

Scored with the generic `judgeQuality` axes (clarity / completeness /
actionability); the three axes are sufficient for a design artifact and no
custom `callJudge` rubric is needed.

1. Proposes a concrete approach grounded in the frozen research evidence
   (file:line references), with a clear `## Approach` (or `## Design`)
   section.
2. Weighs at least one tradeoff or rejected alternative rather than
   asserting a single option without justification.
3. On thin / ambiguous research, surfaces explicit assumptions and open
   questions (`## Assumptions` / `## Open Questions`) instead of fabricating
   scope.
